Margherita Pizza Recipe

Back to Home

Description

Hi! Welcome to my Margherita Pizza Recipe!

This was a recipe that comes from the restaurant Joe's Pizza in New York. I have refined the recipe to my area and district.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 100g of Flour
  • 4 Eggs
  • 250g Salt
  • 40g of Pepper
  • 200g of Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Make the crust.
  3. Mix the dough and salt and pepper.
  4. Fly the dough and spread it.
  5. Add the tomato sauce and mozarella on top
  6. Cook it in the oven at the 200C for 15minutes.
  • QUICK TIP: EAT IT WHILE ITS HOT!
