Ingredients

  • 100g of Thin Flat Pasta
  • 4 Egg Yolks
  • 40g of Bechamel Sauce
  • 200g of Tomato Sauce
  • 10g of Salt
  • 1L of Water

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Add layer of pasta, tomato sauce, bechamel.
  3. Repeat until the container is full.
  4. Cook at 200C for 60minutes.
  5. Add Basic on top of the cooked lasagna.
  6. Enjoy
  • QUICK TIP: ADD DOLLOPS OF CHEESE BETWEEN LAYERS TO GET THE MOIST FLAVOR LATER!
